Chapultepec Forest: Nature and Culture

Chapultepec Forest, situated in the heart of Mexico City, stands as one of Latin America’s most vibrant urban parks. Its rich history dates back to ancient times, with traces of its past preserved within Mexico’s history over the last five centuries. Today, it serves as a vast and green oasis while also hosting important monumental and cultural sites, including the National Museum of Anthropology, Chapultepec Castle, and countless museums.

In early 2019, after decades of tradition, a 1000-acre master plan was initiated to restore and enrich Chapultepec. This park now celebrates all things of nature and fosters sustainable growth in environmental restoration, urban infrastructure, history, archeology, and more.
