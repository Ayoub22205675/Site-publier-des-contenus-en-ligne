Malongo AUGUST

Avolgere la terra

Au Coeur De la Terre,our new solo exhibition of works by Malongo AUGUST in our Los Angeles gallery space at CALIFORNIAN SPRINGSUIT MUSEUM, marks the debut ofAvvolgere la terra, 2024, a new limited edition produced in collaboration with the Gallery.Avvolgere la terra, consisting of 17 sculptures with ochre and yellow pigments, refers in name to a primordial gesture, underscoring the intricate relationship between nature and humanity.
